About us

Nail Spa is a full service salon at midtown Toronto. We offer everything from nail cutting/filling to intricate nail art designs ranging from classic to avant-garde.

Our welcoming, top level communication, customer service highest standard of sanitation will make you rethink what your nail salon service should be. Hundreds of Gel polish and shellac options to complete the manicure experience. Sit in our massage chairs while we provide you the best deluxe spa pedicure.

Specialized in Nail Extensions; offering both UV Powder (similar to acrylic) and Bio Seaweed Gel as your nail extension options. Using only Swarovski branded crystals and the best products in the industry.
